# Industry Playbooks

SO should adapt to real industry behavior instead of using one generic storefront shape. These playbooks describe the default direction for common SO presets.

## Beauty And Wellness

- Prioritize product education, ingredient or benefit storytelling, and visual calm.
- Use soft editorial layouts, strong imagery, and trust signals.
- Keep claims grounded and easy to verify.
- Favor product education sections, routines, and usage guidance.

## Electronics

- Prioritize specs, comparison, compatibility, and performance proof.
- Use clear hierarchy, strong technical naming, and direct CTAs.
- Surface details that reduce purchase friction, such as key features and included items.
- Keep the layout efficient and information dense without becoming cluttered.

## Home

- Prioritize room context, lifestyle imagery, and collection-based browsing.
- Support categories, sets, and visual merchandising.
- Make dimensions, materials, and care information easy to find.
- Use a balanced editorial and shopping mix that works for both inspiration and decision making.

## Playbook Rule

Playbooks should guide default content, imagery style, and section selection, but they must still be flexible enough to support specific merchant brands and product lines.
